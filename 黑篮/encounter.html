That is where we met.

This is a simple coincidence, but is also what makes miracle happen.

It was basketball that contributed to our meeting.

  • Akashi Seijuro

    I was brought up in a gentlefolk, accepting elite education and learning various skills. Basketball is what my late mother has left to me, and that is why I insist on playing basketball. I enjoy using my analyzing ability to win championship in the basketball game. My talent makes me find out special talent of others.

  • Midorima Shintaro

    I have talent in playing basketball, and I enjoy it. But in fact, playing basketball was just a way of relaxing for me, at the beginning. Once I make up my mind to do something, I will try my best. 'Do one's level best and leave the rest to God's will.' That is my favorite proverb.

  • Aomine Daiki

    Basketball is my favorite. I can't remember the first time I have been interested in playing basketball. It just like I was born with the talent and love of basketball.

  • Murasakibara Atsushi

    I don't enjoy playing basketball. I play basketball just because of my height. It was because of my outstanding height that I was asked to participate in Mini Basketball when I was young, and that was the first time that I have been exposed to basketball. I hate being a loser, so I don't want to lose in the basketball match.

  • Kise Ryota

    I like sports, and I have talent in sports, what makes me feel bored. As it is so easy for me to pick up a kind of sports, I always feel bored of it in a very short time. But when I saw Daiki play basketball, I thought he is so powerful and talented, maybe he could be the one I can't surpass easily, and that made me interested in basketball.

  • Kuroko Tetsuya

    I like playing basketball, but I am not talented. Although I have practiced for a long time, my basketball skill is still poor. I am hestitaing whether I should give it up, but I still want to keep the promise I made with my friend in my childhood.
