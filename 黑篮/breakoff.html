Our Broken Relationship

The legendary team finally disbanded after graduation, and so the friendship of the team members did.

Talent Thoughts "Best"
The growing talent of the members made them become prominent in the basketball matches, which made them feel it easy to win games and lose interest and passion in playing basketball.
The excellent talent made them unwilling to show their team work. They started to believe in "Victory is the top priority", and tended to win by themselves instead of team work.
The fact that they should graduate from Teko Junior High school made the disblandation of the team unchangeable. And every member in the team agreed undesignedly that they should be admitted into different high schools and show who is the best player by competing in the high school basketball matches.
